# Code of Conduct

Watchmark uses a lightweight contributor covenant:

- Be respectful and specific.
- Assume good intent, but repair harm quickly when it happens.
- Keep public issues free of credentials, private URLs, and non-public page content.
- Discuss technical tradeoffs with evidence and reproducible examples.

Maintainers may hide, edit, or remove comments that are abusive, spammy, or unsafe for users.
