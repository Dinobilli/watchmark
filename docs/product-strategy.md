# Watchmark Product Strategy

## Positioning

Watchmark is an open-source website change monitor with AI-style summaries. The wedge is Korea-first monitoring: public-sector notices, procurement pages, subsidy deadlines, shopping product pages, and competitor pages.

## Why not just ask AI?

AI can summarize a page once. It does not automatically:

- Remember the previous version.
- Decide whether a change is meaningful.
- Ignore recurring boilerplate.
- Keep a history.
- Alert a team.
- Provide audit evidence for when something changed.

Watchmark owns the workflow and data layer around repeated monitoring.

## Open-source core

- URL checking and normalization.
- Meaningful text extraction.
- Deterministic local summaries.
- Self-hosted dashboard.
- Tests and fixtures for noisy page changes.

## Hosted SaaS path

- Scheduled monitoring.
- Team workspaces.
- Email/Slack/Kakao/Discord alerts.
- Longer history retention.
- AI summaries for complex Korean pages.
- Watch templates for common public-sector and shopping pages.

## First users

- Small ecommerce operators watching competitor prices and product pages.
- Korean freelancers and small businesses watching public notices.
- Founders watching competitor landing/pricing/hiring pages.
- Communities tracking public-interest pages.
